Are you looking forward to settling down in Poland? Poland is a vibrant and exciting country. Living in Poland has not always been a picnic. Today, however, life in Poland is very pleasant, with people who are nothing but sociable and welcoming.

Settling in Poland

To obtain a temporary residence permit, you should provide documented reasons for your living in Poland. Taking up a job or education in Poland, as well as marriage to a Polish citizen, are one of the most common reasons. A temporary residence permit can be obtained for any period, but not longer than 3 years. The application for temporary residence can be submitted at any time in a Polish consulate at your place of residence or in a voivodeship office in Poland.

Study in Poland

If you are an EU/EEA student you are not required to pay tuition fees for courses taught in Polish but you compete with Polish students for admission spots. If you are not from an EU country, studying in Poland requires you to pay tuition fees. The good news is, that prices in Poland are lower than in many other European countries.
